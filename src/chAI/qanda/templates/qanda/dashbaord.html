Grading Objective Questions with AI and Symatic Analysis
chAI is an AI solution for grading objective questions. It uses advanced symantic analysis, including cohere, to accurately evaluate student responses.
How it works [at a glance]
Submit Questions with Answers
To get started with chAI, simply upload your set of objective questions and the corresponding correct answers.
User Answers the Question
Next, have your students or users answer the questions. They can do this through your platform or by manually entering their responses.
Marking and Analysis of Answers
Once the answers have been submitted, chAI uses advanced symantic analysis, including cohere, to evaluate the quality of the responses. We then provide a detailed analysis of each answer and mark the questions accordingly.
